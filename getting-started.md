# Getting Started

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit MegaBridge
2. Click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top right corner
3. Select your preferred wallet (MetaMask, OKX, Rabby, etc.)
4. Approve the connection in your wallet

## Step 2: Choose Bridge Direction

MegaBridge supports two directions:

### Bridge In (Base → MegaETH)
- Send ETH from Base network to receive ETH on MegaETH
- Click the arrow between networks to switch direction if needed

### Bridge Out (MegaETH → Base)
- Send ETH from MegaETH network to receive ETH on Base
- Click the arrow between networks to switch to this direction

## Step 3: Enter Amount

1. Enter the amount of ETH you want to bridge
2. Review the quote showing:
   - Estimated output amount
   - Slippage fee
   - Bridge fee
   - Estimated time (~30 minutes)

## Step 4: Execute Bridge

1. Click **Bridge Funds**
2. Your wallet will prompt you to switch to the correct network if needed
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
4. Wait approximately 30 minutes for the transfer to complete

## Step 5: Receive Funds

Once the bridge is complete, your ETH will appear in your wallet on the destination network.
