# FAQ

## General Questions

### What is MegaBridge?
MegaBridge is a cross-chain bridge that allows you to transfer ETH between Base and MegaETH networks quickly and securely.

### Is MegaBridge safe?
Yes. MegaBridge uses a secure architecture with smart contract deposits and manual fulfillment. Your private keys never leave your wallet, and all transactions require your explicit approval.

### What wallets are supported?
MegaBridge supports MetaMask, OKX Wallet, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, Rainbow, Phantom, Zerion, Bybit Wallet, Bitget Wallet, and any WalletConnect-compatible wallet.

## Bridging Questions

### How long does bridging take?
Bridge transfers typically complete within **30 minutes**. This includes transaction confirmation and manual fulfillment by the bridge operator.

### What are the fees?
MegaBridge charges a 0.1% bridge fee plus 0.05% slippage. You'll also pay gas fees on the source network. See the [Fees](fees.md) page for details.

### Is there a minimum or maximum amount?
- **Minimum**: No minimum deposit
- **Maximum**: 100 ETH per transaction

### Can I cancel a bridge transaction?
Once a transaction is confirmed on the blockchain, it cannot be cancelled. Please review all details carefully before confirming.

## Troubleshooting

### My wallet won't connect
1. Make sure your wallet extension is installed and unlocked
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Check if you have any popup blockers enabled
4. Try a different browser

### I don't see my funds after bridging
1. Wait the full 30 minutes for processing
2. Make sure you're checking the correct network in your wallet
3. Add the destination network to your wallet if needed
4. Contact support if funds don't appear after 1 hour

### Transaction failed
1. Check if you have enough ETH for gas fees
2. Make sure you're on the correct network
3. Try increasing the gas limit in your wallet
4. Wait a few minutes and try again

### Wrong network error
Click the network switch button in MegaBridge - it will automatically prompt your wallet to switch to the correct network.
